## ðŸŒŸ About Splendly

Splendly is a fast, elegant, and privacy-conscious expense tracker designed to work **offline by default**.  

- Track expenses & income locally on your device  
- Visualize spending trends and manage categories  
- Minimal data is sent to Firebase for **analytics & crash reporting**  
- No ads or unnecessary tracking  

> **Note:** Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics collect anonymized usage and crash data to help improve app stability and features. **No personal transaction data is uploaded.**
